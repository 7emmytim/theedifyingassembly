About Us

We are an in-Christ ministry and a bible training center, fully committed to the word of the risen Christ as revealed in the scriptures through teachings and demonstrations.

We believe that God's will for men is what Christ has done in his death, burial and resurrection and this should be proclaimed in every land and exalted in every heart.
To this end, we are devoted to raising and training ministers who will play their part in ensuring the will of the Father is not impossible.

Layhold Outreaches

Layhold outreaches are periodic evangelistic avenues with which we reach targeted loacalities with the purpose of stirring up men to embrace and be ignited for the ministry of Jesus.
Follow us to know when there will be an outreach near you.
